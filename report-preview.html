Your Answer: B) 50 Hz

Question 42: What is the primary purpose of a load bank test?
Your Answer: C) To clean the generator
Correct Answer: A) To verify the generator can handle its rated capacity
Explanation:
Load bank testing verifies a generator's ability to handle its rated capacity by applying artificial electrical loads. This testing identifies potential issues before they cause failures during actual power outages.
