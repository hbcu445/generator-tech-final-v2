- Centered

### Colors:
- Background: Clean white or very light cream
- Text: Dark navy (#1e3a5f) for headings, black for body
- Accent: Could add subtle gold/yellow elements matching logo

### Border:
- Optional: Elegant border frame (thin double-line or decorative)
- Navy color to match branding

### Spacing:
- Generous white space for professional appearance
- Well-balanced vertical spacing between sections

### Professional Elements:
- Clean, formal typography
- Proper alignment (everything centered)
- Underlines for fill-in fields
- Professional signature lines
